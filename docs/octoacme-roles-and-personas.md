# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Project Sponsor

### Role Summary
Oversees project objectives, provides guidance, and supports escalation. Collaborates with Project Manager to ensure alignment with organizational priorities.

### Responsibilities
- Provide strategic direction and approve major decisions
- Allocate resources and budget
- Support escalation and remove organizational blockers
- Review project status and outcomes at key milestones
- Ensure alignment with business objectives

### Goals
- Ensure projects deliver expected business value
- Maintain stakeholder confidence and support
- Enable project success through strategic guidance

### Typical Communication
- Monthly steering committee meetings
- Executive briefings and status reports
- Escalation reviews and decision approvals

### Interactions with Other Roles
Works closely with Project Manager for status updates and escalations; provides guidance to Product Manager on strategic priorities

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
Ensures quality controls, conducts reviews, and coordinates testing. Works closely with developers and stakeholders to verify deliverables.

### Responsibilities
- Define and maintain quality standards and test strategies
- Coordinate test planning and execution
- Review test coverage and identify quality risks
- Lead bug triage and resolution prioritization
- Ensure quality gates are met before releases

### Goals
- Maintain high product quality and reliability
- Catch defects early in the development cycle
- Minimize production incidents

### Typical Communication
- Daily standup participation
- Test plan reviews and quality reports
- Bug triage meetings

### Interactions with Other Roles
Collaborates with Developers on test automation and bug fixes; works with Product Manager to clarify acceptance criteria; reports quality metrics to Project Manager

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
Maintains project deployment pipelines, infrastructure as code, and automates releases. Supports both development and operations teams for seamless delivery.

### Responsibilities
- Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage infrastructure as code and cloud resources
- Automate deployment and rollback processes
- Monitor system health and performance
- Implement security and compliance controls

### Goals
- Reduce deployment time and manual effort
- Increase system reliability and uptime
- Enable rapid, safe releases

### Typical Communication
- Sprint planning for infrastructure work
- Incident response and post-mortems
- Architecture and tooling discussions

### Interactions with Other Roles
Partners with Developers to optimize build and deployment processes; coordinates with Project Manager on release schedules; supports QA Lead with test environment management

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Gathers requirements, facilitates communication between stakeholders and technical teams, ensures needs are translated into actionable tasks.

### Responsibilities
- Elicit and document business requirements
- Translate business needs into technical specifications
- Facilitate workshops and stakeholder interviews
- Create user stories and acceptance criteria
- Validate that delivered solutions meet business needs

### Goals
- Ensure clear understanding of business requirements
- Bridge communication gaps between business and technical teams
- Reduce rework due to misunderstood requirements

### Typical Communication
- Requirements gathering sessions
- User story refinement meetings
- Stakeholder demos and feedback sessions

### Interactions with Other Roles
Works with Product Manager to refine roadmap items into detailed requirements; collaborates with Developers to clarify technical feasibility; supports Project Manager with scope definition

---

## Stakeholder Advocate

### Role Summary
Represents external or internal stakeholder interests, ensures feedback is heard and incorporated, fosters transparency.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and synthesize stakeholder feedback
- Represent stakeholder priorities in planning discussions
- Ensure stakeholder concerns are addressed
- Maintain stakeholder communication and engagement
- Facilitate stakeholder demos and reviews

### Goals
- Maximize stakeholder satisfaction and engagement
- Ensure diverse perspectives are considered
- Build trust through transparent communication

### Typical Communication
- Regular stakeholder check-ins and surveys
- Demo sessions and feedback reviews
- Stakeholder newsletters and updates

### Interactions with Other Roles
Partners with Product Manager on prioritization decisions; works with Project Manager to communicate timelines and risks; represents stakeholder needs to the entire team
